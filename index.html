Privacy Policy – StarHail

Effective Date: June 22, 2026

StarHail does not collect, store, or share any personal data directly. The game does not require user registration and does not collect personal information such as name, email address, or location.

However, the app uses third-party services that may collect information automatically to provide their services.

Advertising

StarHail uses third-party advertising services (such as Google AdMob) to display rewarded ads. These services may collect certain data automatically, including:

  • Device information
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Usage data

This information is managed directly by the advertising provider and is subject to their respective privacy policies.

In-App Purchases

The game offers optional in-app purchases processed securely through Google Play. Payment information is handled directly by Google and is not accessed or stored by StarHail.

Google Play Services

The app may use Google Play Services, which may collect data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. For more information, please review:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Children's Privacy

StarHail is not specifically directed at children under 13 years of age. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
